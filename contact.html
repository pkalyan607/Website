Reach IIIT Kottayam

IIIT Kottayam permanent Campus is located in Valavoor village, Pala, Kottayam. The Campus is about 10 km away from Pala which is en route to IIIT Kottayam.

From the Airport

IIIT Kottayam is about 70 km from the Cochin International Airport, Nedumbassery. A taxi will cost around Rs. 2000/- from Airport to IIIT Kottayam campus.

From the Railway Station/KSRTC Bus Station

IIIT Kottayam is about 40 km from Kottayam Railway Station/KSRTC Bus Station. KSRTC (Kerala State Road Transport Corporation) bus services are available from KSRTC Bus Station to Pala.From Pala You can hire an auto/taxi or othewise you can opt for private buses to Valavoor Junction & then you can hire an auto to IIIT Kottayam campus.
